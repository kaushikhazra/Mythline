# Relevance Evaluation Rubric

Evaluate if the research output contains relevant information about the research topic.

## Evaluation Priority
1. **If file_content is not null/empty**: Evaluate the file_content primarily
2. **If file_content is null/empty**: Evaluate the research_summary

## Criterion
Does the output contain relevant information about the research topic asked in the query?

## Scoring
- **1.0**: Directly answers the research query with relevant information
- **0.5**: Partially relevant but missing key aspects of the query
- **0.0**: Off-topic or does not address the research query
