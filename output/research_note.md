## Magistrix Landra Dawnstrider's Chain - Story Summary

This quest chain centers on the blood elves confronting the creeping Scourge corruption in Eversong Woods.

- **The Wayward Apprentice**: Search for Apprentice Mirveda investigating forbidden research near the Dead Scar.
- **Corrupted Soil**: Collect tainted soil samples from East Sanctum to study the Scourge's influence.
- **Unexpected Results**: Defend Mirveda from a Scourge ambush; realize the corruption may be irreversible.
- **Research Notes**: Deliver Mirveda's findings to Magistrix Landra Dawnstrider, acknowledging the urgent threat to their land.

The storyline explores environmental devastation, the blood elves' fight against the Scourge, and rising desperation.
