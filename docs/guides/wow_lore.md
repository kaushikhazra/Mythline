## Go Take Candle! / The Final Candle
Go Take Candle! and The Final Candle are rogue-class quests in The Ringing Deeps involving stealing candles from an inn; they require stealing multiple candles (timed) and are part of a candle-themed quest chain. These quests feature stacked candles and stealth mechanics rather than controlling NPCs.

## Kobold Candles
Kobold Candles is an early Elwynn Forest quest where players collect large candles dropped by kobolds in nearby mines (Fargodeep/Jasperlode). Candles and candlewax are common quest items and decorations in mines and small caverns.

## Stolen Torches
Stolen Torches is a quest item type found on bandits/defias in some zones; torches and candle piles are frequently used as environmental props in inns, mines, and caverns.

Source: Web searches on 2025-11-15


## Kobold Candles (Elwynn Forest - Fargodeep / Jasperlode Mines)

Kobold Candles is a level-7 Elwynn Forest quest (giver: William Pestle) that asks players to collect Large Candles dropped by kobolds in the Elwynn mines (notably Fargodeep and Jasperlode). Kobold mine interiors and side caverns commonly feature stacked candles, lit torches and smoky air; many community maps place notable kobold areas and the Goldtooth/kobold camp around coordinates near 42,58 in the Fargodeep/Jasperlode mine region. Source: Web search on 2025-11-15 (Wowpedia, TwinHead, JudgeHype).
## Northshire Abbey

Northshire Abbey is the Brotherhood of Northshire's monastery in Northshire Valley (Elwynn Forest). The abbey includes a Main Hall and chapel-like interiors with stained-glass windows and a warm, candlelit atmosphere used in quest scenes and novice training. Source: Web search on 2025-11-15

## Brother Paxton

Brother Paxton is a human librarian/scribe and quest NPC associated with Northshire Abbey, commonly found in the library or main hall inside the abbey. He is a Brotherhood of Northshire caretaker and appears in early Elwynn Forest quests (quest-completion scenes). Source: Web search on 2025-11-15

## Northshire Abbey Chapel (Northshire Valley)

The Northshire Abbey chapel is a small indoor chapel inside Northshire Abbey with wooden pews, candles, and stained-glass windows; interiors are typically lit with warm, golden candlelight and have a peaceful, devotional atmosphere appropriate for priest NPC interactions and quest completions.

Source: Web search on 2025-11-15

## Milly Osworth
Milly Osworth is an NPC in Northshire Valley (Elwynn Forest) and owner of Northshire Vineyards; she appears in Classic-era quests such as "Milly's Harvest" and "Grape Manifest." The vineyard sits near Northshire Abbey and features low stone walls and rows of grapevines that can be used as an in-game backdrop for vineyard scenes.

Source: Web search on 2025-11-15

## Milly Osworth

Milly Osworth is a human NPC quest giver located in Northshire Valley, Elwynn Forest. She is the owner of the Northshire Vineyards located on the eastern edge of Northshire Valley near the path to Goldshire; quests associated with her include "Milly's Harvest" and "Grape Manifest." The vineyard area features long rows of grapevines and is commonly used in early-level Elwynn Forest quests.

Source: Web search (Wowpedia, Sage Guide) on 2025-11-15

## Milly's Harvest

Milly's Harvest is a low-level (level 4) Alliance quest in Elwynn Forest given by Milly Osworth at Northshire Abbey. The quest concerns Defias bandits raiding the Northshire Vineyards (grape trellises, buckets/crates of grapes) southeast of the abbey; players recover her harvest crates/buckets. Source: Web search on 2025-11-15
