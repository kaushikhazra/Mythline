Research Story

Tell me about Shadowglen
Shadowglen is the starting area for Night Elf characters in World of Warcraft. It is located on the great tree Teldrassil, serving as the peaceful training grounds for young Night Elves beginning their journey.
What quests are available there?
Shadowglen features several introductory quests including "The Balance of Nature" and "A Good Friend." These quests introduce players to the druidic traditions of the Night Elves and the threats facing their homeland.
